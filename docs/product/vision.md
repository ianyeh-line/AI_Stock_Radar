# Product Vision

AI Stock Radar helps Taiwan stock investors turn scattered market information into a concise, explainable, and actionable daily decision.

## Problem

Investors face too much information every morning:

- US market movement
- AI and semiconductor news
- Fed and macro data
- Taiwan market sentiment
- Company-specific events
- Technical signals
- Institutional flow

The problem is not lack of data. The problem is lack of decision clarity.

## Solution

AI Stock Radar converts market information into a structured decision flow:

```text
What happened?
Why does it matter?
Which Taiwan stocks are affected?
Is the technical setup confirmed?
What is the final action?
```

## Target User

Taiwan stock investors who need a fast but disciplined morning decision process.

## Product Promise

Every morning, complete the Taiwan stock decision process in 3 minutes.
